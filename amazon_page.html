What's the difference between the iPad Pro and iPad Air?
An iPad Air and an iPad Pro differ in that the Pro has a larger screen size, an extra 10MP ultra-wide camera in addition to the 12MP main camera that the Air also has, Face ID, compared to the Air's Touch ID, and four speakers, compared to the Air's studio speakers. In terms of performance, the Air and Pro are identical, with a Apple M1 processor and 8GB RAM.
